Welcome to the User Study!

Introduction

Thanks for participanting in our user study. During the study you would use a visualization recommendation tool to complete 4 tasks, each task takes about 5-15 mins to complete.
After each task, there would be a short post-task questionnaire.
Time stamps are important for our research. Please do not get interrupted or leave during each task. There will be a stop page after each post-task questionnaire. Feel free to use the stop page to take a break if you need to.
Attention: Participants will be excluded from the study if they rush through tasks or they are inactive for an unreasonable long term in any of the tasks. Excluded participants will not be compensated.
